Choking: Perform the Heimlich maneuver or seek immediate veterinary attention.
Poisoning: Identify the substance and contact a vet or animal poison control.
Severe Injuries: Control bleeding, immobilize the injured area, and rush to the vet.
Heatstroke: Move to a cool place, provide water, and seek immediate veterinary care.
Electric Shock: Disconnect the source, check for breathing and pulse, and rush to the vet.
